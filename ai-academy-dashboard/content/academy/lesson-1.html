4 AI Landscape 2026

❌ Year 2023

  • Human → AI → Output
  • AI as assistant
  • Hallucinations as main problem
  • Prompt engineering is everything

✅ Year 2026

  • Agent → Agent → Agent → Output
  • AI as autonomous actor
  • Autonomy vs. control as challenge
  • Architecture and governance matter most
